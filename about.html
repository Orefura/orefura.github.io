This site serves to standardize all fan translations for Orefura, primarily the character and place names, to follow the current active translation, Defiring's version. There are also corrections on various mistranslations, both major and minor, that are spinkled throughout the older translations.

All translations used will be properly credited and linked to the original translators' sites.
